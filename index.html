How to use this calendar

  1. Set your goal name, amount, date range, and working days, then click Save.
  2. Click any day cell to add, edit, or delete entries (labels and amounts).
  3. Your data is saved locally in your browser on this device.
  4. Use Reset All to clear everything and start fresh.
  5. Switch between light/dark themes in Appearance.
